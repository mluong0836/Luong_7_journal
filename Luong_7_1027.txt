1. The process of creativity is to use fundamental skills to create a more intricate and unique design; therefore distinguishing it from the other projects.

2. The steps involved would be:

	First you need to come up with a plan.
	Next you start applying your main plan and start adding in or removing things that you decide you don't want later. 
	Then you test your project to see if there are any errors.
	If there are errors, you need to go back and fix them through tinkering.
	After your project works, start adding things that makes your project unique.
